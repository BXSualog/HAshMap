Version 1.1.0 (Major Update)
Date: 2026-03-30

Summary of Updates:
Revolutionized the Alerting System with Forecast-based Detection and Comprehensive Background Monitoring. The app now scans the 5-day forecast for upcoming typhoon threats and monitors both typhoons and high heat index hazards even when the app is closed.

Key Features:
- Upcoming Typhoon Detection: Automatically scans for Signal #1–5 threats in your location's 5-day forecast.
- Periodic Weather Status: Sends a regular weather update (e.g., "Sunny, 30°C") every 30 minutes.
- Intelligent Alert Throttling: "Upcoming" and "Heat Index" alerts are limited to once every 6 hours to prevent notification fatigue while maintaining safety.
- Detailed Signal Context: Active notifications now include specific PAGASA-based signal descriptions and primary safety tips.
- Background Heat Warnings: Extended the background thread to monitor and notify you of dangerous heat conditions (25°C+) without needing to open the app.

Modified Files:
- services/storageService.ts: Added persistent notification tracking (NOTIF_TRACKER).
- services/notificationService.ts: Added `sendUpcomingTyphoonNotification` and enhanced existing alert templates.
- hooks/useWeather.ts: Integrated forecast scanning logic for real-time foreground updates.
- tasks/backgroundWeather.ts: Overhauled the background worker to support forecast monitoring and heat hazard detection.
